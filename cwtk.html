CHRISTMAS WITH THE KAROUNTZOSES

Feature Film (2015)

Michael Karountzos invites his family over to celebrate, expecting a promotion at work. But when the promotion falls through, Michael realizes he will have to spend Christmas Eve with “the relatives.” However, by the end of the night, he realizes there is no better place to be than with family.
